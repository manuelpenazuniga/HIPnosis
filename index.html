01 · ORACLE, NOT OPINION

A real build loop

Compiles on AMD silicon, measures every fix by the compiler, and reverts what doesn't help. Success is declared by the build and the numbers — never by asking an LLM.

02 · THE SILENT BUG

Wave64 divergence

AMD wavefronts are 64 lanes, not 32. A __ballot_sync(0xffffffff) compiles clean and computes wrong. HIPnosis flags it statically — zero false positives on real kernels.

03 · PAPERS AT THE BORDER

The Port Passport

Every run emits a hash-signed attestation of the diff, the verdict and the environment. Verifiable by anyone with sha256sum — no trust required.

Radical honesty is the point. The demo replays a recorded run, and the badge always says so — synthetic, mock, or live silicon. We never show a scripted terminal claiming a GPU result that didn't happen.
